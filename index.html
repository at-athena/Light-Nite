Light Nite Tokenomics

Introducing the cornerstone of Crypto Blades: our tokenomics. Dive into the heart of the game's economy and discover how our unique token structure drives the adventure, enabling you to forge your path to crypto rewards.

Token

Token Name: LITE
Total Supply: 500 Million LITE tokens

Utility

In-game Purchases: can use to acquire skins and other assets
- Trading: LITE tokens can be traded for other in-game assets or swapped on crypto exchanges.
Staking: Players can stake LITE tokens to earn rewards
- Battle Pass: Access to seasonal battle passes

Initial Distribution

- 35% for public sale
- 20% for Team & Developers (Vested for 18 months)
- 15% Game Reserves
- 15% Marketing & Partnerships
- 10% Community & Tournaments
- 5% Advisors

Security

Periodic audits to maintain the integrity of the LITE token and its associated smart contracts.

Burn Mechanism

A percentage of LITE tokens spent on certain in-game assets may be burnt, aiming to reduce total supply and potentially increase token value.

Rewards

Players can earn LITE tokens through victories, completing in-game missions, or excelling in special events.

Future Developments

Plans to integrate with other gaming ecosystems or introduce gaming NFTs, enhancing the overall player experience and diversifying LITE token utility.

About Light Nite & What We Do

Light Nite: Embracing the fervor of the renowned battle royale gameplay from Fortnite, Light Nite brings a thrilling twist to the crypto sphere. Set in a vibrant digital universe, players navigate an expansive world where every move and strategy is intertwined with the project's native cryptocurrency. Light Nite isn't just about survival; it's about mastering the art of blockchain-based gaming. Engage in adrenaline-pumping battles, trade in-game assets, and experience a dynamic economic ecosystem, all while enjoying the benefits and potential rewards of cryptocurrency integration. Ready, set, dive into the future of gaming with Light Nite!
